About Bounty Board

What is this?

Getting a job can be frustrating. It can be especially frustrating when you don't know where to look. At Bounty Board, we are trying to make that a little easier.


Here, you just need to know what you're looking for. We'll check multiple databases for you, and then bring all the results back and put them in a format that's easy to understand.

How To Use

It's pretty simple! Just put in the keywords you're looking for, like "java" and "developer", the location you're looking in, and click 'Search Jobs.' That's all you have to do!


If you find that you're getting a lot of results from one database that aren't useful, don't worry! Click on the 'Customize Job Boards' button to open a menu of all the available databases. Select ones you want results from, and de-select ones that you don't. Click 'Save Changes' and you're ready to go!
